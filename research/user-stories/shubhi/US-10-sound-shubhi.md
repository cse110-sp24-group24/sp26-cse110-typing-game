# US-10 - Audio Controls - Sprint 1

## Customizing the game environment

**As a CS student**, I want to control the game’s music and sound effects so that I can create an environment that helps me focus.

---

## Acceptance criteria

- [ ] Players can adjust music volume
- [ ] Players can adjust sound effect volume separately
- [ ] Players can mute all audio
- [ ] Audio settings remain saved after restarting the game

---

## Notes

Students may play in shared spaces or while studying, so flexible audio controls are important.

## Dependencies

- Audio system must support adjustable volume settings
- Save system must store preferences

## Definition of done

Players can customize and save their preferred audio settings.
