# US-07 - High Score Tracking - Sprint 1

## Measuring improvement over time

**As a CS student**, I want the game to save my best scores so that I can track my typing progress and stay motivated.

---

## Acceptance criteria

- [ ] Highest score is saved locally
- [ ] Best words per minute is recorded
- [ ] Results screen highlights new records
- [ ] Saved scores remain after closing the game

---

## Notes

Visible improvement helps motivate students to continue practicing.

## Dependencies

- Save system must store player statistics
- Results screen must compare previous scores

## Definition of done

Players can view their saved personal best statistics across multiple sessions.
