# US-05 - Motivation - Sprint 2

## Tracking class-week progress milestones

**As a new CS student**, I want weekly milestones tied to beginner programming topics, so that I can feel steady progress during my first quarter.

---

## Acceptance criteria

- [ ] Milestones are grouped by week themes (variables, conditionals, loops, functions)
- [ ] Completing relevant waves fills a weekly progress tracker
- [ ] Finished milestones unlock a small badge or title
- [ ] Progress resets to a new set each academic week

---

## Notes

Students often stay engaged when gameplay aligns with class cadence. Weekly goals can make practice feel directly connected to coursework.

## Dependencies

- Topic tagging system must exist for snippets or waves
- Profile layer must persist milestone state by week

## Definition of done

Players can view and complete week-based topic milestones that update as they play and refresh on a weekly cycle.
