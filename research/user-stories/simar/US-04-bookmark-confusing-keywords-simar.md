# US-04 - Study Support - Sprint 1

## Saving unfamiliar terms while playing

**As a new CS student**, I want to bookmark confusing keywords during a run, so that I can review them later when I am not under time pressure.

---

## Acceptance criteria

- [ ] Player can mark a word or line as "review later" with one hotkey
- [ ] Bookmarked items are saved at end of run
- [ ] Review list includes snippet context and language label
- [ ] Player can remove reviewed items from the list

---

## Notes

Typing games move quickly, and beginners cannot pause to study every unknown token. Bookmarking supports focused review after gameplay.

## Dependencies

- Snippet data must expose line-level identifiers
- End-of-run screen must surface stored bookmarks

## Definition of done

During play, users can bookmark confusing content and access a persistent review list after the run.
