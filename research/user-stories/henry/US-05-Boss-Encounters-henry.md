# US-05 · Boss Encounters · Sprint 2

## Fighting high-difficulty boss enemies

**As a player**, I want boss fights with longer and harder snippets, so that major milestones feel exciting and memorable.

---

## Acceptance criteria

- [ ] Bosses appear after milestone waves
- [ ] Boss snippets contain advanced syntax
- [ ] Bosses have multiple health phases
- [ ] Unique music plays during boss fights
- [ ] Players receive rewards after victory

---

## Notes

Boss fights should feel tense and require sustained focus.

## Dependencies

- Wave progression system
- Advanced snippet pools

## Definition of done

A player can encounter, defeat, and receive rewards from a boss encounter.
