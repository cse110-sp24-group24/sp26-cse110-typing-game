# US-08 · Dynamic Audio System · Sprint 3

## Increasing music intensity during danger

**As a player**, I want audio intensity to react to gameplay situations, so that the atmosphere feels immersive.

---

## Acceptance criteria

- [ ] Music intensity increases during high danger
- [ ] Boss encounters trigger unique tracks
- [ ] Combo streaks affect sound effects
- [ ] Audio transitions occur smoothly
- [ ] Players can adjust audio settings

---

## Notes

Reactive audio improves emotional engagement and pacing.

## Dependencies

- Audio playback engine
- Gameplay event triggers

## Definition of done

Players hear dynamic audio changes tied to gameplay intensity.
