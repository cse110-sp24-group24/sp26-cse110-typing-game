# US-10 · Health and Failure System · Sprint 2

## Penalty for typing mistakes

**As a player**, I want typing mistakes and missed enemies to reduce my health, so that the game feels challenging and mistakes have consequences.

---

## Acceptance criteria

- [ ] Player health is displayed on screen
- [ ] Incorrect typing can reduce health or apply penalties
- [ ] Enemies reaching the bottom also reduce health
- [ ] Game ends when health reaches zero

---

## Notes

Without meaningful consequences, players may ignore accuracy entirely. The health system creates tension and encourages focused typing.

---

## Dependencies

- Health tracking system
- Enemy collision/failure detection system

---

## Definition of done

Typing mistakes and missed enemies affect player survival, and the game ends correctly when health reaches zero.
