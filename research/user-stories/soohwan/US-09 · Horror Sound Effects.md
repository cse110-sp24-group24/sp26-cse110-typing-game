# US-09 · Horror Sound Effects · Sprint 2

## Audio feedback during gameplay

**As a player**, I want sound effects tied to enemies and typing actions, so that gameplay feels more immersive and satisfying.

---

## Acceptance criteria

- [ ] Enemy defeat sound plays when enemies are cleared
- [ ] Incorrect typing triggers an error sound
- [ ] Ambient horror background audio loops during gameplay
- [ ] Audio volume levels remain balanced and non-overlapping

---

## Notes

Audio is essential for reinforcing the haunted atmosphere. Sounds should enhance tension without overwhelming the player.

---

## Dependencies

- Audio playback system
- Enemy event system

---

## Definition of done

Gameplay contains responsive sound effects and ambient horror audio that function consistently during play.
