# US-08 · Enemy Speed Scaling · Sprint 2

## Increasing difficulty over time

**As a player**, I want later waves to become progressively harder, so that the game remains challenging as my skills improve.

---

## Acceptance criteria

- [ ] Enemy spawn rate increases over time
- [ ] Typing complexity increases in later waves
- [ ] Difficulty progression feels gradual rather than sudden
- [ ] Early waves remain beginner-friendly

---

## Notes

The game should ease players into difficulty before becoming demanding. Abrupt difficulty spikes would frustrate beginner players.

---

## Dependencies

- Wave management system
- Snippet difficulty categorization

---

## Definition of done

Each new wave increases gameplay difficulty in a noticeable but manageable way.
